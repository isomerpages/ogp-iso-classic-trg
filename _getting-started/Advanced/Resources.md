<p><strong>A Resource Room is the equivalent of the newsroom</strong>, and
it comes with features that categorises the contents for you.&nbsp;Your
posts are sorted by date and you can filter them by year using the date
filter (Shown Recent)</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/resource_room.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>Every isomer site can only have 1 resource room. This can be created under
Resource room in IsomerCMS.</p>
<p>Note that once the resource room is named, it <strong>cannot be changed anymore</strong>.
You would have to contact Isomer support for help.</p>
