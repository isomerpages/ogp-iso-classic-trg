<h1>Review request</h1>
<p>Review request is mandatory for your site collaborators to thoroughly
assess changes before granting approval and publishing the site. You can
start by clicking on “Request a Review” in your workspace or site’s dashboard.</p>
<p></p>
<h3>How do I raise a review request?</h3>
<p>On your site’s dashboard, click on “Request a Review”</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/review1.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>You can fill in any details about the changes you have made and choose
at least 1 approver. Only users with an Admin role can approve a review
request.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>It is recommended to choose more than one reviewer for instances where
1 reviewer is not present to approve.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Screenshot_2024_08_20_at_7_14_46_AM.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>You can preview the changes in the review request through the "Edited
Items" tab.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/view_edited_items.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>Click on “Submit Review” and your reviewers would receive an email about
the review request.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/reviewemail.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<h3>How do I approve?</h3>
<p>You cannot approve your own review request. Only tagged reviewers can
approve a review request.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/approve1.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>Change the "In review" status to "Approved" to approve the changes. Note
that other reviewers can un-approve an approved request.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/approve2.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>Once approved, you will see this success message.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/approve3.png">
</div>
