<h2>Same same but different</h2>
<p>When you check out isomer websites, you'll notice that while their layouts
are similar, each one has its own unique twist. This is because they use
different sections to build their homepage, almost like assembling with
Lego blocks. The blocks are predefined, but you can arrange them in various
ways to create a distinct look.</p>
